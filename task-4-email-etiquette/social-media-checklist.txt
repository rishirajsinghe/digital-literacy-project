$ Do’s

Think before posting; assume everything online is permanent
Keep your profile professional and respectful
Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication
Verify information before sharing it
Respect others’ opinions, privacy, and boundaries

$ Don’ts

Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly
Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content
Do not engage in online arguments or trolling
Do not click on suspicious or unknown links
Do not overshare your location or daily routine
